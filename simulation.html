    Offline Flow

    Offline mode reads the correct hierarchy, overlays the chosen case drift, and writes JSON to `artifacts/offline` so the graph can animate without reaching AWS.

    1. Pick a named case such as `test1` or `test2`.
    2. Generate offline artifacts with `python app.py export --offline --demo-drift --test-case test1 --out-dir artifacts/offline`.
    3. Open this page and click `Move Users` to watch the correction path.
